FAQ,s

  • In depth conversation on your vision, sentimental value and expectations for the book
  • We will present samples to inspire ideas and refine the concept.
  • Clarify the book's purpose and theme: Journey through decades, family history, travel adventures etc.
  • Decide on the tone: nostalgic, celebratory, heartfelt or fun!
  • Discuss the preferred size, cover style, and binding!

  • Create a base document when you start uploading all the pictures and other tokens and write personal anecdotes.
  • We can reach out to others on your behalf to collect input for the book.
  • Incorporate quotes, poetry, or words from significant figures if desired.

  • Collaboratively outline the sections of the book (e.g., childhood, achievements, travels)
  • Sequence photographs and stories to chronicle the journey effectively.
  • Suggest places for artwork, captions, and highlights for significant events.

  • We create initial layouts, integrating materials with creative visuals.
  • Present digital proofs for your review and feedback.
  • Adjust designs according to your preferences (colours, fonts, motif).

  • Provide a final digital proof for comprehensive review.
  • Make any final tweaks or edits requested.

  • Choose premium printing materials and finishes based on your selection.
  • Coordinate the process with a vendor.

  • Package the book elegantly, suitable for gifting if requested.
  • Offer delivery options.

  • Provide care instructions for preserving the book.
  • Offer reprints, additional copies, or digital versions if desired.
